# Stay Safe Online Checklist
## Actionable Safety Tips
* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone
* Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
* Always verify the authenticity of calls or messages
* Use strong, unique passwords for each account
## UPI / ONLINE PAYMENT SAFETY
* Never approve payment requests from unknown sources.
* Use only trusted and official payment apps (such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.).
* Always verify the receiver’s details carefully before doing any transaction.
* Remember: You do not need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
